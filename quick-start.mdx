## Prerequisites

Before diving in, ensure you have:

- A valid email address for account verification
- Access to a modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card or payment information is required to get started. Our database of 1362 dry cat foods from over 300 brands is free to explore.
</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Follow these steps to set up your Cat Food Central account in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [https://catfoodcentral.com](https://catfoodcentral.com) and click **Sign Up** in the top right.

    Fill out the form with your email, a strong password, and your name.

    Click **Create Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Check Your Email" icon="mail">
    Open your inbox and find the verification email from Cat Food Central.

    Click the **Verify Email** button in the email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the site and log in with your credentials.

    You'll land on the personalized homepage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication in your account settings for added security after your first login.
</Callout>

## Perform Your First Food Search

Use the search bar to find dry cat foods by brand or ingredient.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="By Brand" icon="package">
    <Step title="Search Purina" icon="search">
      Type `Purina` in the search bar and hit Enter.

      Review results like Purina Pro Plan Shredded Blend Chicken & Rice.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Apply Filters">
      Use side filters for `<30%` carbs or `>40%` protein.
    </Step>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="By Ingredient" icon="leaf">
    <Step title="Search Chicken" icon="search">
      Enter `chicken` to see foods with real chicken as the first ingredient.
    </Step>

    <Step title="Sort Results">
      Sort by rating or AAFCO statement compliance.
    </Step>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Example search query:
````javascript
// Simulated search parameters
const searchParams = {
  query: "chicken meal",
  minProtein: 40,
  maxCarbs: 25
````

## Save Favorite Products

Keep track of top choices for your cat.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add to Favorites" icon="heart">
    On any product page, click the **heart icon** next to the name.

    Example: Save "Orijen Original Dry Cat Food".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Favorites" icon="bookmark">
    Click **Favorites** in the navigation bar.

    Review, compare, or share your list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Remove Items">
    Click the filled heart to unfavorite.
  </Step>
</Steps>
