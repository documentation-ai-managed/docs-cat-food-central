title: What is the food rating?
description: Explanation of Cat Food Central's rating system.
---

## Overview

Cat Food Central gives every dry food a score from 1 to 10.

## How is it calculated?

- Ingredient quality
- Protein and fat percentages
- Preservatives used
- Brand transparency
- User reviews impact scores

## Where to see it?

Each food profile shows its rating at the top next to its name.
