title: Cat Food Not Found
description: What to do if your cat food brand or product is missing.
---

## Symptoms

- Search returns "No results"
- Food brand or product page missing

## Possible reasons

- New product not added yet
- Typo in search

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check spelling">
    Try alternate spellings or parts of the name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request addition">
    Email us a link or photo at `support@catfoodcentral.com` and we will add it within 48 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>
