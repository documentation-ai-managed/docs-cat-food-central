---

## Overview

Easily compare two or more dry foods for ingredient and nutrition details.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find foods">
    Use the search bar to locate your cat foods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add to Compare">
    Click the "Compare" button on each food's page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Comparison">
    Go to the top bar and click "Compare" to view side-by-side nutrition, ingredient quality, and ratings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
