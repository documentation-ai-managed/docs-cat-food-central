<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.3.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Features

- Added 150 new dry cat food products from emerging brands like Whisker Wellness and Purrfect Nutrition, bringing the total to 1512 products.
- Introduced advanced filtering by protein source (chicken, fish, beef) and grain-free options.

## Improvements

- Enhanced search algorithm to prioritize user-rated foods and AAFCO compliance.
- Updated evaluation scores with new metrics for ingredient quality and recall history.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed sorting issues in brand comparison views.
- Resolved mobile UI glitches during product detail scrolling.

</Update>
