## Advanced Search Filters

Narrow down the vast database using precise filters for nutrition, price, and ratings.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Nutrition" icon="apple">
    Filter by protein content, fat levels, and fiber. For example, select foods with protein `{>30%}` and fat `{<15%}` for active cats.

    ```javascript
    // Example filter query
    const filters = {
      proteinMin: 30,
      fatMax: 15,
      fiberRange: "2-5%"
    };
    ```

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Prioritize crude protein over guaranteed analysis for accurate comparisons.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Price" icon="dollar-sign">
    Set budgets per pound or per bag size. Compare value across premium and budget brands like Purina or Royal Canin.

    | Price Range | Example Brands | Avg Rating |
    |-------------|----------------|------------|
    | `<$2/lb`   | Iams, Purina  | 3.2/5     |
    | `$2-4/lb`  | Hill's, Blue Buffalo | 4.1/5 |
    | `>$4/lb`   | Orijen, Acana | 4.7/5     |
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Ratings" icon="star">
    Sort by overall score, ingredient quality, or species-appropriateness. Only view foods rated `{>4 stars}`.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Detailed Food Breakdowns

Dive into individual products to understand what you're buying.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search and Select" icon="search">
    Enter a brand like "Wellness CORE" and apply filters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Ingredients" icon="list">
    Scroll to the ingredient list. Look for named meat sources first, avoiding corn or by-products.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze Nutrition" icon="activity">
    Check the guaranteed analysis panel for protein, fat, and carb estimates (calculated as 100% minus protein + fat + fiber + ash + moisture).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quality Ratings and Criteria

Our ratings combine expert analysis and user feedback.

<Callout kind="info">
  Ratings range from 1-5 stars. Higher scores favor whole prey ingredients, minimal plant fillers, and balanced nutrition mimicking a cat's natural diet.
</Callout>

Key criteria include:

- **Ingredient Quality** (40%): First 5 ingredients must be animal-based.
- **Species Appropriateness** (30%): High protein, low carb `{<20%}`.
- **Product Quality** (20%): No artificial additives.
- **Helpers** (10%): Probiotics, omega fatty acids.
