<Callout kind="note" collapsed="false">
  Coverage is **dry cat food only**, scored against what cats actually need as
  obligate carnivores: high protein, low carbohydrates, and quality animal-based
  ingredients.
</Callout>

## What you can do here

- **Browse and search** 1,400+ dry cat food products

- **Advanced search** - filter dry cat food by nutrition and traits like grain-free

- **See what's actually in your cat's food** - beyond the marketing claims on the bag

- **Learn about feline nutrition** through guides on protein, carbohydrates, ingredients, and more

## How scores work

Every product is evaluated using the same 9-rule methodology, based on its ingredient list and guaranteed analysis - not price, brand reputation, or availability. The maximum score is 100. The higher the score, the better the food aligns with a cat's natural dietary needs.
