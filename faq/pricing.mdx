<Callout kind="note" collapsed="false">
  Every cat parent deserves clear, unbiased information about what they're feeding
  their cat. The core of Cat Food Central will always be free.
</Callout>

## How does the site sustain itself?

Cat Food Central may use general advertising or affiliate links to cover running costs. These never affect scores or rankings - see [Independence](/faq/independence) for details.
